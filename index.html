Autobiography

Hello there! My name is Jan Neal Isaac D. Villamin, or you can just call me Isaac. I was born in Manila on January 16, 2003. I was raised in the City of Santa Rosa, Laguna for the majority of my life. I spent 19 years there until I had to leave for Los Baños in Scholars Dormitory to attend the University of the Philippines Los Baños, as a computer science student.


My life mainly centered on my passion for mathematics. I started out in Grade 2 when I luckily qualified for a math program. I loved problem-solving from the get-go. I had many hobbies throughout my lifetime, such as singing, gaming, playing the guitar, and drumming, but none of them took more of my time than when I engage in mathematics. I’ve trained under different schools and programs like MTG and MOSC and competed in different countries. I was able to achieve my high-school goal of qualifying for the Philippine Mathematical Olympiad in Grade 12.


Until all of these turned-down when I discovered the field of computer science. One of my friends invited me to join the National Olympiad in Informatics Philippines and fell in love with algorithms as soon as I solved my first ever problem. Since then, I switched my favorite hobby from stressful mathematics to stressful competitive programming. Currently, I am trying to develop my web development skills by participating in organization-related work.
